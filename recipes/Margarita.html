Blue Margarita

These incredibly refreshing margaritas are SO easy!They only require 4 ingredients, and no blender/cocktail shaker!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Tequila
  • ⅓ Cup Blue Curaçao
  • ⅓ Cup Triple Sec
  • ⅔ Cup Lime Juice Freshly Squeezed (The Juice From ~8 Small Limes).
  • Crushed Ice

Steps

  1. In a large pitcher, combine tequila, triple sec, blue curacao, and lime juice. Stir until well combined.
  2. Fill glasses with ice. Pour drinks over ice and enjoy responsibly!
