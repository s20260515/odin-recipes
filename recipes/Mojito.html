Mojito

This cool and refreshing mojito recipe is the perfect way to beat the heat.

Ingredients

  • 7 limes approx. 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • ½ cup mint leaves
  • ¾ cup simple syrup or to taste, recipe
  • 1 cup white rum
  • club soda
  • ice

Steps

  1. Juice the limes into a 2L pitcher.
  2. Add the mint and muddle/squish really well to release the flavors of the mint.
  3. Add 1 cup of rum and ¾ cup of simple syrup or more to taste.
  4. Fill the pitcher with ice, top with club soda, & stir.
